What We Do

A song built entirely from what you share.

Every MemoVivo song begins with a conversation — a few questions that invite families to share the moments, phrases, and details that made someone who they were.

From those stories, we compose original lyrics and produce a full song. Not a template. Not a generic tribute. A song that could only ever be about this one person.

Working alongside funeral homes, hospice providers, and assisted living communities, MemoVivo helps the people you love be remembered the way they lived.

1

Share the Memories

Families answer a few warm, simple questions — a favorite moment, a phrase they always said, a song they loved.

2

We Compose the Song

Your responses become the lyrics. Every verse, every chorus is built from what you shared — nothing generic, nothing borrowed.

3

Delivered Within 2 Days

A finished song with a permanent sharing link, a formatted lyric document, and a family sharing kit — ready to play at the service or keep forever.

4

Yours to Keep

MP3, high-quality WAV, and a printable lyric sheet designed for framing. A gift that will still mean something twenty years from now.
